This Privacy Notice for Cardinal App LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you:

  • Download and use our mobile application (Cardinal), or any other application of ours that links to this Privacy Notice

Get reminders before your cards expire, access barcodes for easy in-store scanning, and never waste gift card value again. With Premium features, you'll even receive location-based alerts when you're near stores where you have available gift cards. Cardinal makes gift cards as easy to use as Apple Pay.
